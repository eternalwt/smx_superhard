Silicon PCD

PCD

  • Our company has successfully developed silicon thermally stable PCD and wear resistant & heat-resistant conductive PCD. And we can ensure stable product quality through strict control of raw materials, scientific and reasonable proportioning design and parameter control.
  • At present we can supply PCD mix size φ40×24mm with diamond powder 10μm-40μm. Special size can be tailor-made according to customer's requirements.

Silicon thermally stable PCD characteristics

  • High temperature resistance, good thermal stability (Heat resistance temperature can reach 1100。C).
  • High wear resistance, the abrasion ratio is more than10×104.
  • Super low friction coefficient, good heat conduction performance and low thermal expansion coefficient.
  • Corrosion resistance (All kinds of acid, alkali, salt), strong anti-oxidation and light weight.

Main Applications

  • Wire drawing dies: processing and drawing ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals, making rod, tube and wire.
  • Corrosion resistant and wear resistant parts: oil nozzle, water cutting and sand blasting nozzle, drill bit protection, oil seal and bearing, etc.

Wear resistant & heat-resistant conductive PCD characteristics

  • Good conductivity, it can process with slow wire cutting technology.
  • High temperature resistance (Heat resistance temperature can reach 1000。C).
  • Good wear resistance, the abrasion ratio is more than6×104.
