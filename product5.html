Multi blade diamond micro-powder

Features

  • Many cutting edges. There are hundreds of sharp cutting edges on each particle, the removal efficiency is very high.
  • Good self-sharpening, appropriate pressure can break it and form a new cutting edge.
  • The production process is more environmentally friendly than the detonation method polycrystalline diamond, the cost is lower, the surface accuracy of the processing objects is higher.

Its difference from nanometer polycrystalline diamond

  • Nanometer polycrystalline diamond powder is composed of aggregated nano particles, grinding mainly rely on the point cutting of the small nano particles’ convex surface; but multi cutting edges diamond can do linear cutting, so the multi cutting edges diamond has higher cutting efficiency and higher removal efficiency.
  • Nanometer pocrystalline diamond powder choose detonation polycrystalline diamond as raw material, it is produced through crushing, shaping, grading and purifying; multi cutting edges diamond powder choose ordinary diamond powder as raw material, it is produced by special chemical etching method which makes the particle surface a large amount of blade like cutting edges. In comparison, the production process of multi cutting edges diamond powder is more environmentally friendly.

Application fields

  • High precision and rapid grinding and polishing of crystal and optical instruments such as sapphire, monocrystalline silicon, polycrystalline silicon, silicon carbide, glass ,etc.
